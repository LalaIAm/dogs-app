# Product: RoadDoggs

RoadDoggs is a road trip companion app ("The Un-Map") targeted at adventurous travelers who prefer off-the-beaten-path exploration over conventional navigation. The brand identity is anti-corporate, analog-inspired, and community-driven.

## Core Value Proposition

- Smart POI discovery that surfaces hidden gems (not chain restaurants)
- Route building with a focus on the journey, not just the destination
- Pack/community features for group road trips
- Offline-capable for areas with no signal

## Brand Voice

- Rebellious, playful, lo-fi aesthetic
- Taglines: "Get Lost (but like, on purpose)", "The Anti-Grid Manifesto", "Don't just drive. Roam."
- Visual language: scrapbook, analog film, polaroids, post-it notes, handwritten annotations

## Current Phase

The project is in early development (beta vol.3). The immediate focus is building the marketing landing page as a single-page React application to communicate brand identity and drive beta signups.
